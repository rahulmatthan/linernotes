Common Issues

  • Apple Music playback not starting: Confirm you are signed into Apple Music and your subscription is active.
  • Song not available: Some tracks vary by region/storefront. Re-select from search if playback fails.
  • Hunt not appearing: Pull to refresh hunt list and check network access.

Response Time

We aim to respond to support requests within 2 business days.
